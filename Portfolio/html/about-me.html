about me
Hi, I’m Aaron. I’m a developer who loves turning ideas into clean, fast, human-friendly products. I’m most at home in frontend and full-stack work, with a growing focus on practical AI. I’m always looking for new challenges and for other builders who care as much as I do—whether that’s starting a study group, shipping a weekend project, or making everyday tools simpler so you have more time for what you love.

My Story

I started college aiming to become a neurosurgeon, fascinated by how the brain works. Along the way, I shifted into engineering and eventually gravitated to code. A line from a book stuck with me: When you're so absorbed that time itself forgets you, that's where passion lives.That was programming for me.

I didn’t switch majors—I built my skills the hard way: extra courses, side projects, and lots of late-night tinkering. I even tossed my Industrial Engineering résumé and walked into a national career fair as a developer. That gamble paid off with my first software engineering role.

What drives me

  • Keep learning, keep shipping
  • Build with care and make complex things feel simple
  • Find (and be) the kind of teammate who elevates everyone

Faith, passion, and consistency got me here—and they’ll take me further. I want to keep growing, building, mentoring, and contributing to teams that believe in me as much as I believe in the work.

If my story resonates with you—or if you’re looking for a collaborator—let’s connect and build something worthwhile!
